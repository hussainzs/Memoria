Purchase to Plate – National Average Prices for NHANES 	
By: Andrea Carlson, Christopher Lowe, and Elina Page
	
Recommended Citation: U. S. Department of Agriculture, Economic Research Service. Purchase to Plate Suite, March 2023. 	
	
Table of Contents	
PP-NAP1112	These prices are constructed from the IRI InfoScan 2013 sales and are linked to NHANES 2011/2012 food codes.
PP-NAP1314	These prices are constructed from the IRI InfoScan 2015 sales and are linked to NHANES 2013/2014 food codes.
PP-NAP1516	These prices are constructed from the IRI InfoScan 2015/2016 sales and are linked to NHANES 2015/2016 food codes.
PP-NAP1718	These prices are constructed from the IRI InfoScan 2017/2018 sales and are linked to NHANES 2017/2018 food codes.
	
	
Variables:	
Year			Indicates the data period of NHANES where the food_code is found
food_code		Code assigned for the food in the USDA nutrition databases, including the foods reported in NHANES
mod_code		Code assigned to a modification of a food_code. Use food_code and mod_code when linking to the FNDDS (PP-NAP 2011/2012 only)
food_description	Description of the food associated with food_code
method			Number of the method used to calculate the price
method_description	Name of the method used to calculate the price (the publications under “Price Construction” in the Additional Literature section of the Purchase to Plate data product page on the ERS website provide additional details) 
NHANES			Indicates why the food_code is included in the PP-NAP (not in PP-NAP 2011/2012):
				o Base – Food identified at the beginning of the price creation process
				o Top 90 – Participants in WWEIA/NHANES, reported eating this food or beverage at least 10 times (PP-NAP 2013/2014 only)
				o Extra – Has a direct link to IRI grocery data and exists in the FNDDS only or the food was added later in the process to facilitate the next revision of the USDA Food Plan market baskets
				o Alcohol – Alcoholic beverage (PP-NAP 2013/2014 only)
price_100gm		The price per 100 edible grams of the food in nominal dollars for the year of the IRI data used to estimate the price

	
Acronyms used in this data file:	
altEC	a pricing method used for FNDDS codes that were not linked using the Purchase to Plate Crosswalk – prices for these unmatched FNDDS codes were imputed by using the price(s) from one or more matched FNDDS codes
FNDDS	Food and Nutrient Database for Dietary Studies
NFS	Not further specified
NHANES	National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey
NS	Not specified
PP-NAP	Purchase to Plate National Average Prices 
SR	USDA National Nutrient Database for Standard Reference (used through PP-NAP 2015/2016)
WWEIA	What We Eat in America
	
	
Source: USDA, Economic Research Service (ERS); based on data from USDA, Agricultural Research Service (ARS) and Information Resources, Inc. (IRI).
